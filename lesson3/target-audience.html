Target Audience

The target audience for the site are people interested in getting to know more about business opportunities in San Lorenzo, and motivated to help the economy grow through collaboration and active participation, as consumers or investors.

Personas

Persona 1: John Investor

John Investor is a 55-year old experienced businessman looking for opportunities to help others grow their business and extend their reach. He is always looking for producers that would need assistance to develop new markets, increase in sales, and connect with other businesses in the same field.

Persona 2: Anna Consumer

Anna Consumer is a 40-year old member of the community who likes to know more about the companies available in the city. Her family is middle-class and she is used to collaborate with new entrepreneurs. She is also an entrepreneur herself, and she is interested in making her products known to the public.

Scenarios

  • Are there investment opportunities in San Lorenzo?
  • What are the most thriving companies in the city?
  • What kind of products or services are most prominent?
  • What days are best to set meetings and meet business owners?
  • Who are the people in charge of helping businesses grow?

About Us

Bountiful was created in 1997 by James and John Doe, in hopes of blending two of their greatest passions together: health, and delicious, fresh fruit. Working with national producers and international suppliers of exotic fruit, they have been able to deliver top-quality products with affordable, customer-friendly prices.

The city of Carlsbad is the perfect place to locate the project: beautiful beaches, warm weather, and young people that love freshly squeezed juices. That's why they started everything at Ocean Street: to give everyone the best Carlsbad can offer. Come and enjoy a nutritious and delicious drink with us today!
